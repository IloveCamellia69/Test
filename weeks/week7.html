Picha Kucha Presentation

Pecha Kucha Reflection:

Describe the experience?

The experience was presenting what I had created up until week 3 of summative 2, week 7 of the term.

Describe the feelings and thoughts you had about the experience?

Before the presentation I felt that I was woefully under prepared for what I was about to undertake and lack experience which doubled down on the fact that I was under prepared. During the presentation I felt anxious due to being under prepared. And when the presentation ended I felt relieved that it was over. my thoughts on this experience was, from the beginning, that I stumble on more then a fair share of my stuff and that the presentation wasn’t going to be that great. But after presenting and improvising everything, i felt really good that I got it over and done with as I did. In the end I thought it went pretty decently

What was good and bad about the experience?

What was good about the experience was that I knew my project well enough to not need anything to help me with the presentation. The bad thing was that I still hated presenting and didn’t want to do it so I became unprepared for it.

Why was it good or bad? Why did things happen this way?

It was goo for me to know my own project because with the base foundation of the project I can properly articulate what the project is about from my own mind without having to read it, or memorized it. Which leads to less confusion on what the solution was. This happened because I was the one who conducted the research of the project and the solution behind it. I knew who my target audience was and i know what their pain points are, so I was about to have a great understanding and how to communicate this understanding on a moments notice.

What did you learn about this situation.

I’ve learnt that even with little preparation for the presentation, you can still present your ideas and your research to back up these ideas if you put in the effort and actually doing things in a why where you understand it well enough to come to a good solution for the problem.
