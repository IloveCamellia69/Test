Problem statment:

How can we find the best solution to help reduce crime and try to solve the underlaying issues of Massey?

Personas:

Some ideas for solutions:

  • Big brother to watch over everything and control everyone
    • This idea would be fun to go through with but the implication of it would be catastrophic.
    • This could turn peoples view of ai worse then it already is turning to a dystopia.
  • Urban planning project
    • Urban planning project have turned a run down area into around with great benefits to its current and future residents.
    • it would also give the place a more lively feel while providing for the community itself with either new jobs or a way to for people to relieve stress for to have some fun.
  • Community projects/gathering/activities
    • Community projects also do a lot of good, it lifts the spirit of its inhabitants and their pride of the community
    • but these dont really change the overall state of the community because they are normally small and only involve the community itself rather then the local council to make a major impact to change the community for the better.

Final Idea

The final idea ive chosen as a solution as an Urban planning project.

  • This is because i feel that the plan would make the neighborhood look and feel much safer for people to live in
  • It would also provide areas for people to hang out like skate parks, general park, walking areas and more activates for people to do. And i feel that this has great potential to help teenagers to not vandalize buildings but providing them a place for them to do so.
  • it would also life peoples perception of the community give its inhabitants more pride and to take care of the community better in the future.

Research Board

When looking at my project and what it needs to represent(and Urban development project) I looked for architectural websites as the mainly deal with buildings and relates to the project. So seeing how other people choose to design their websites would allow me to create a similar feel.

Mood Board

After creating the research board and seeing how architects design their websites, I get the feel of it being a little different for my area. It feels too clean and simple with little to no identity behind it. And looking at a map there are a lot of greenery and parks in the area so I want to incorporate that into the feel/mood of the design to recreate the feel of the current Massey and to reinforce that the greenery will still be there in the new development of Massey.
